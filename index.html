Gudetama

A loveable and lazy egg

Here's a time line of Gudetama's life:

  • 2012 - Created by AMY, a 26 year old Sanrio designer.
  • 2013 - Placed second in a Sanrio competition.
  • 2014 - Made debut on a TBS TV program.
  • 2014 - Village Vanguard, a NYC restaurant, opened a limited-time Gudetama-themed cafe.
  • 2016 - EVA Air, a Taiwanese international airline,launched a Gudetama Jet Comfort Flight.
  • 2016 - Holika Holika, a Korean cosmetics brand, launched a Gudetama-themed cosmetics line.
  • 2016 - Shoryu Ramen teamed up with Gudetama to launch an autumn limited edition menu.
  • 2016 - Stayreal and Gudetama released a limited edition line of apparel.
  • 2017 - TBS morning program featuring Gudetama airs its 1000th episode.
  • 2018 - Seibu Railway collaborated with Gudetama to launch a Gudetama-themed train.
  • 2019 - Stayreal launches a second apparel line in collaboration with Gudetama.
  • 2019 - UNIQLO and Gudetama collaborate to launch limited edition T-shirts.
  • 2019 - Sentosa, an island in Singapore, received more than 800 Gudetama inflatables for their annual festival.
  • 2020 - Launched a limited-edition of top ramen with Nissin Foods.
  • 2020 - Followed by 8092 people on Twitter.
  • 2020 - Planning to take over the world!
